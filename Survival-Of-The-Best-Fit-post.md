### Survival Of The Best Fit Learning

### 1. What was the motivating factor or factors during the game which resulted in the integration of a Machine Learning model to evaluate job candidates through automated resume review?
   
The company was growing and to meet demand they needed to quickly hire employees, The hiring manager was given a short time frame to hire the employees.  To meet the demand, it was decided to use Machine Learning model to hire the candidates by training the model on previous hires.

### 2. What “good” situations existed in the game?
   
Having the Machine Learning model scan through hundreds of resumes quickly to identify candidates to hire help speed up the hiring process.

### 3. What “bad” situations existed in the game?
   
The speed of hiring caused bias in the decision making and I focus more on education and skills and caused other qualities to be overlooked.  Using my previous hiring selection, caused the Machine Learning model to inherit my bias traits.    When I reached out to IT they stated the leaning model used by hiring traits and if their bias is came from me unknowingly. 

### 4. How would you advise the business leader about when and how to implement a Machine Learning model to automate or improve the throughput of a hiring process?
   
I would advise the using Machine Learning models to help improve time consuming process like reviewing resumes to find potential candidates is a great help to hiring managers.  I would strongly advise not to allow the model to decide the candidate to hire but to recommend and have a human review the recommendation.   As with this game, that you may train the model to be bias to a particular type of candidate.  By doing human review, it allows for models to be retained to remove bias decisions.
